### Introducing AksharA: Free AI-Powered Handwriting Learning App for Children

We are excited to announce the public release of **AksharA**, a completely free, interactive web application designed to help children learn handwriting through AI-powered recognition technology.

#### What is AksharA?

AksharA is an educational platform that makes learning to write fun and engaging for children. Using advanced artificial intelligence, the app recognizes children's handwriting in real-time, providing instant feedback and guidance as they practice writing alphabets and numbers.

#### Key Features

**For Children:**
- **Interactive Drawing Canvas** - Draw letters directly on screen using mouse or touch
- **Instant AI Feedback** - Know immediately if you wrote the letter correctly
- **Fun Learning Modes** - Choose between relaxed Practice mode or challenging Test mode
- **Master Every Letter** - Smart corrected tests ensure children practice until they get it right
- **Colorful & Engaging** - Child-friendly interface designed to keep kids motivated
- **Works Everywhere** - Use on computers, tablets, or mobile phones

**For Parents & Educators:**
- **Progress Tracking** - See scores and grades for each test
- **Multilingual Support** - Currently supports English alphabets and Tamil vowels
- **Safe & Private** - No data collection, all learning happens in your browser
- **Completely Free** - No subscriptions, no ads, no hidden costs
- **Open Source** - Transparent, community-driven development

#### Who Can Benefit?

- **Pre-school children** learning to write their first letters
- **Elementary students** improving handwriting skills
- **Special education** settings requiring interactive learning tools
- **Homeschooling families** looking for engaging educational resources
- **Teachers & educators** seeking supplementary teaching aids
- **Parents** wanting to support their child's learning journey

#### How It Works

1. **Choose Your Mode** - Practice for casual learning or Test for assessment
2. **Draw Letters** - Use your finger or mouse to write on the digital canvas
3. **Get Feedback** - AI instantly recognizes and evaluates your writing
4. **Keep Practicing** - Automatic corrected tests help master difficult letters
5. **Track Progress** - View scores and celebrate improvements

#### Technology for Good

Built with cutting-edge web technologies including Next.js, TensorFlow, and Tesseract OCR, AksharA demonstrates how artificial intelligence can be used to democratize quality education and make learning accessible to all children, regardless of their economic background.
